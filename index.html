good2way

good2way is an offline aviation phrasebook, FIR reference and coordinate conversion app for pilots.

Features

  • Offline aviation phrasebook
  • FIR reference by region
  • Favorites and groups
  • Personal notes
  • Coordinate converter
  • Optional regional database unlocks

good2way is a briefing, training and reference aid only. It does not replace official aeronautical publications, charts, NOTAMs, ATC instructions, company manuals or regulatory documents.
