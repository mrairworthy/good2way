Privacy Policy

good2way is designed as an offline-focused aviation reference app. Privacy is part of that design.

Effective date: July 2026

good2way is developed and operated by Free Speed Studio, based in Germany. The app is designed as an offline-focused FIR/UIR phrasebook, aviation reference and coordinate conversion app.

01

Data Collection

good2way does not require a user account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number or location.

The app does not display third-party ads and does not use advertising tracking.

02

Notes, Favorites and Groups

good2way allows users to save favorites, custom groups and personal notes inside the app. These entries are personal to the user.

Notes, favorites and groups may contain personal information if the user chooses to enter it. Free Speed Studio does not require users to enter personal information into these fields.

03

iCloud Sync

If iCloud Sync is enabled on the user's device, notes, favorites and groups may be stored and synchronized through the user's Apple iCloud account using Apple services.

Free Speed Studio does not operate its own server for this data and does not have access to the user's iCloud account or iCloud-synced app data.

04

In-App Purchases

good2way offers optional non-consumable In-App Purchases to unlock regional FIR/UIR datasets or the WORLD unlock.

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Free Speed Studio does not receive, process or store payment information such as credit card numbers or billing details.

05

Analytics and Tracking

good2way does not use third-party advertising trackers.

Apple may provide developers with aggregated App Store analytics and sales reports. Such information is provided by Apple and handled according to Apple's own privacy policies.

06

External Links

The app or this website may contain links to external websites, for example support, legal or informational websites. External websites are outside the control of Free Speed Studio and may have their own privacy policies.

07

Operational Disclaimer

good2way provides non-binding, reference-only aviation information for familiarisation and convenience. It is not intended for operational use and does not replace official aeronautical publications, charts, NOTAMs, ATC instructions, company manuals, regulatory documents or approved training material.
