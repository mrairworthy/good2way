Privacy Policy for good2way

Effective date: July 2026

good2way is developed and operated by Free Speed Studio.

good2way is designed as an offline aviation phrasebook, FIR reference and coordinate conversion app. We respect your privacy and do not collect, sell or share personal data.

1. Data Collection

good2way does not require a user account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number or location.

The app does not use advertising tracking and does not display third-party ads.

2. Notes, Favorites and iCloud

good2way allows users to save favorites, groups and personal notes inside the app.

If iCloud is enabled on your device, this data may be synchronized through Apple iCloud using Apple’s services. Free Speed Studio does not have access to your iCloud data.

3. In-App Purchases

good2way offers optional non-consumable In-App Purchases to unlock regional FIR databases or the WORLD unlock.

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Free Speed Studio does not receive or store your payment information.

4. Analytics and Tracking

good2way does not use third-party advertising trackers.

If Apple provides anonymous App Store analytics, this information is handled by Apple according to Apple’s privacy policies.

5. External Links

The app may contain links to support, legal or informational websites. These websites are outside the app and may have their own privacy policies.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
