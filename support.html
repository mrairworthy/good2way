Quick help

If audio does not play, please check Silent Mode, device volume and the selected iOS voice. Enhanced or Premium voices can be managed in iOS Settings under Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices.

If notes or favorites do not appear immediately on another device, please make sure iCloud is enabled and allow some time for synchronization.

If a coordinate cannot be parsed, check the hemisphere letters N/S/E/W, remove extra characters and try a simpler format.
