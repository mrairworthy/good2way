Frequently asked

A few useful answers.

For anything else, send an email and include as much detail as possible.

Does good2way require an internet connection?

The app is designed to be offline-focused. Core FIR/UIR reference content and app tools are intended to remain accessible without an active data connection after the relevant content is available in the app.

Do I need an account?

No. good2way does not require a user account.

How does iCloud sync work?

When available and enabled, your notes, favorites and groups may synchronize through your Apple iCloud account. Free Speed Studio does not operate its own server for this data.

How do I restore In-App Purchases?

Open the Store area in good2way and use the restore purchases option. Purchases are associated with the Apple Account used for the original App Store transaction.

Is good2way approved for operational flight use?

No. good2way provides non-binding, reference-only information for familiarisation and convenience. Official and current aviation sources always take precedence.
